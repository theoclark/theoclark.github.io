Welcome

Hi, my name is Theo. I’m a former high school Physics teacher embarking on a career in Machine Learning. It’s a daunting field to break into: filled with super smart and highly motivated people, progress happens at a lightning pace and there are so many frameworks, concepts and models that it can quickly become overwhelming. Yet, I’ve loved every minute of it so far. There’s something so exciting about being part of a field which is really pushing the boundaries of what is possible and driving technology forward every day. Whilst we have to be careful about the risks and limitations of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence in general, the models that have been built are already so capable and impressive that they frequently blow my mind.

As I wade into this field, I’ll try to post here semi-frequently about interesting topics and concepts and ay projects I’ve been working on. If anything interests you and you’d like to reach out then I’d love to hear from you. Feel free to contact me using any of the links at the bottom of the page. Happy reading.
